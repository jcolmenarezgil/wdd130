TEDx

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks. Talks are given by leading intellectuals and creative minds from around the world and cover a wide range of subjects, fostering learning, conversation and connection.

The Church of Jesus Christ

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is a community of people who follow Jesus Christ. They believe in Jesus Christ and follow His teachings. They also believe in the Bible and the Book of Mormon.
